Vegetable Rice

A lovely vegetable recipe full of flavor which can be eaten by itself.
Delicious if eaten with plain yogurt. Create your own variation with
different vegetables and spices.

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time Servings Yield:
10 mins 40 mins 50 mins 2 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 cup Basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon garam masala
  • ¾ cup frozen mixed vegetables

Directions

  1. First Step

    In a large pan heat oil over medium heat and saute onions and cumin
    until onions are tender, but not browned. Rinse the rise several times
    and drain well. Add the rice to the pan with the onions and pour in the
    2 cups of water. Stir in salt, garam masala and vegetables. Cover the pan
    and increase the heat to high.

  2. Last Step

    Bring the mixture to a boil and then reduce the heat to low, keeping the
    rice covered at all times. After cooking for about 10 minutes, gently
    stir the rice to distribute the spices evenly. Cook 25 to 30 minutes, or
    until all of the water has evaporated and the rice is tender.

Pro Tip
